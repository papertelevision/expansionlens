Common Questions

How do I evaluate a location before opening a new business?
ExpansionLens generates a comprehensive location analysis report for any U.S. address. You'll receive an Expansion Score (0–100), competitive landscape map, demographic breakdown, market capacity estimate, and a tailored strategy — all within 15 seconds. It's designed to replace months of manual research with a single, data-backed report.
What is the best site selection tool for small businesses?
ExpansionLens is built specifically for independent operators, franchise groups, and small business owners who need enterprise-level location intelligence without enterprise pricing. At $149 per report, it delivers the same competitive analysis, demographic data, and scoring methodology that large chains use — without the five-figure consulting contracts.
How do I know if an area has too much competition for my business?
Every ExpansionLens report includes a competition density analysis based on the business-to-resident ratio in your area, an interactive competitive map showing exactly where competitors are clustered, individual competitor ratings and reviews, and a clear Upside & Risks breakdown that flags oversaturation. If the area is too competitive, the report will tell you — and suggest what it would take to differentiate.
What data sources does ExpansionLens use?
Reports are built from U.S. Census Bureau data (ACS 5-year estimates) for demographics, Google Places for real competitor ratings and reviews, Walk Score for walkability and transit access, and OpenStreetMap for geographic mapping and points of interest. All data is pulled live at the time of report generation.
How is the Expansion Score calculated?
The Expansion Score (0–100) is a weighted composite of eight factors: population density, household income, competition levels, competitor quality, walkability, education levels, employment rate, and population growth. Each factor is weighted based on its proven impact on business success for your specific industry. A score above 75 indicates a strong expansion target; below 25 signals high risk.
Can I use ExpansionLens for dental practice site selection?
Yes — dental practices are one of our core verticals. The report analyzes dentist-to-population ratios, nearby practice ratings and reviews, patient demographics, income levels that support elective procedures, and walkability for patient access. Every tooltip, strategy recommendation, and scoring weight is calibrated specifically for the dental industry.
Is there a location analysis tool for bars and restaurants?
ExpansionLens offers a dedicated Bars & Nightlife analysis that evaluates foot traffic potential, nightlife competition density, walkability (critical for DUI-conscious areas), local spending patterns, and nearby entertainment anchors. Scoring weights are adjusted for the hospitality industry — for example, walkability carries twice the weight compared to dental, because foot traffic directly impacts bar revenue.
How does ExpansionLens compare to Buxton or Placer.ai?
Buxton and Placer.ai are enterprise platforms that typically require annual contracts starting at $10,000+. ExpansionLens delivers comparable location intelligence — competitive analysis, demographic scoring, market capacity estimates, and strategy recommendations — for a one-time cost of $149 per report. There are no subscriptions, no minimum commitments, and reports are generated in under 15 seconds.
What does a location analysis report include?
Each ExpansionLens report contains 12 sections: an Expansion Score (0–100), Upside & Risks analysis, market capacity estimate, win strategy, interactive competitive map, competitive insight with ratings, key metrics dashboard, demographic profile, points of interest, written analysis summary, nearby competitors table with reviews, and an execution checklist with immediate, short-term, and strategic next steps.
How long does it take to generate a location report?
Reports are generated in real-time — typically under 15 seconds. ExpansionLens pulls live data from multiple sources simultaneously, so you see each step (demographic analysis, competitor scanning, score calculation) as it happens. There's no waiting for a consultant or analyst.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with my report?
If your report fails to generate due to a technical error, you'll receive a full refund immediately. If you are otherwise unhappy with the results of your report, send us a message and we will refund you the cost of the report, simple as that.
What types of businesses can use ExpansionLens?
ExpansionLens currently supports dental practices and bars/nightlife venues, with medical offices, mental health practices, and coffee shops coming soon. Each industry vertical uses custom scoring weights, competition benchmarks, and strategy recommendations tailored to that specific business type.
